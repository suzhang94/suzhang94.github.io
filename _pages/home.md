I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Washington State University in 2025, advised by [Dr. Matthew E. Taylor](https://drmatttaylor.net/) in [the IRL Lab](https://irll.ca). My research focused on reinforcement learning, transfer learning, and learning from demonstration.

I currently work as a **Machine Learning Engineer** at [Nirva Labs](https://www.nirva.life/), where I build AI-powered audio understanding systems — including speaker diarization, voiceprint matching, and LLM-driven content extraction — deployed on AWS with real-time inference.

### Research Interests
Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Human-in-the-loop RL, Agent-based Systems, LLM Applications
